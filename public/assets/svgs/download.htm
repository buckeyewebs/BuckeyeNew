  • Instructions for Student appearing in Terminal Examinations:-
    1. Students are required to download their admit card from CMS and bring printed copy of admit and original student Id card along to examinations hall. The Admit card will be available online on Saturday, June 10, 2023. Students are allowed to appear in exams of only those courses which are appearing in admit card.
    2. Mobile phone / smart watches and any other electronic gadget / device that has function of communication / Store data, books, written material are strictly prohibited to bring into examination hall. Such items, if found in possession of any student during the paper/exam, shall be treated as equivalent to other such material that can be used for cheating and unfair means. These devices/gadgets shall be confiscated and sent to the UMCC which shall decide about the penalties to be imposed to return such items. The invigilator may ask to see / check such devices / gadgets during point of examination.
    3. Student without ID Card and Admit Card is not allowed to sit in the examination room and both documents must be displayed on a corner of table or chair.
    4. Student/candidate is not allowed to enter exams hall after 30 minutes start of exams.
    5. Any Gadgets / Mobile Phones / Smart watches are not allowed inside the examination room. The students are advised NOT to bring them to the university during examination and in case of any damage to confiscated mobile/device, CUI administration will not be responsible.
    6. Students are not allowed to leave the examination hall before one hour has elapsed, anyone leaving the examination hall for any reason shall not be allowed to re-enter.
    7. Students with shortage of attendance are not allowed to appear in the Examination of respective paper.
    8. Students are required to be respectful to invigilators and other officials dealing with the examination process.
    9. No eatables allowed inside examination hall.
